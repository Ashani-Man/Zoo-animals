    Lions

  • Mella
  • Karl

    • Lions are majestic, carnivorous felines known for their golden fur and impressive manes in males. They inhabit savannas and grasslands in Africa and parts of Asia, living in prides led by dominant males. Lions are apex predators, preying on a variety of large herbivores.

    Monkeys

  • Cookie
  • Earl
  • Banana Pudding

    • Monkeys are highly intelligent, agile primates found in various habitats worldwide, from tropical rainforests to urban areas. They come in a wide range of sizes, colors, and species, including baboons, macaques, and capuchins. Monkeys have dexterous hands and feet, allowing them to grasp objects and navigate their surroundings with ease.

    Alligators

  • Wren
  • Aspen
  • Mika

    • Alligators are large, reptilian predators known for their armored bodies, powerful jaws, and broad snouts. Found in freshwater habitats like swamps, marshes, and rivers in the southeastern United States and parts of China, they are apex predators, feeding on fish, turtles, birds, and mammals.
